EXERCICES DE PRONONCIATION

Review the pronunciation of adjectives that come before nouns in the Prononciation section of the previous Préparation before doing the following practice activities.

Exercice 1

Answer the speaker, saying that the item or person asked about is good. Use the adjective bon, good.

Modèle

Tu as des carottes?

Oui, j'ai de bonnes carottes.

Commençons.

Exercice 2

Answer the speaker's questions by saying that the item asked about is the next one. Use the adjective prochain, next.

Modèle

C'est quelle rue?

C'est la prochaine rue.

Commençons.

Exercice 3

The speaker asks for your opinion regarding the following people or things. Say that each person or thing is big, using the adjective grand. Note that when preceding a noun that refers to people, grand usually means great rather than big.

Modèle

Elles sont comment, ces villes?

Ce sont de grandes villes.

Commençons.
