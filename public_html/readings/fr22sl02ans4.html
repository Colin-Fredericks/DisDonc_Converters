ANSWERS

All the forms are different from the corresponding present indicative forms except for the ils-form, which is the same in the subjunctive as in the indicative. 

In the singular, the final consonant of the stem  is pronounced in the subjunctive; it is not pronounced in the indicative. The nous-form ends in ions in the present subjunctive (as opposed to -ons in the present indicative). The vous-form ends in -iez in the present subjunctive (as opposed to -ez in the present indicative).
