EXERCICES ORAUX 19-21

Exercice oral 19: Le piano

Explain how people feel about playing the piano by expressing each English sentence in French. Repeat the correct French equivalent after the speaker.

Modèle

I like to play the piano.

J'aime jouer du piano.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 20: Où habiter?

The following people have experiences and ideas to share about finding an apartment. Express each English sentence in French. Repeat the correct French equivalent after the speaker.

Modèle

I want to find an apartment.

Je veux trouver un appartement.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 21: Les transports en commun

How do these people feel about taking public transportation (les transports en commun)? Express each of the following sentences in French to find out. Repeat the correct French equivalent after the speaker.

Modèle

I hate taking the subway.

Je déteste prendre le Métro.

Commençons.
