EXERCICES ORAUX 8-9

Exercice oral 8: Un enfant difficile

Little Baudoin Jolivet is telling his mother all the things he doesn't want to do. She points out that these things have to be done. Play the role of Baudoin's mother, using il faut to respond to the little boy.

Modèle

Je ne veux pas manger ces haricots.

Mais, il faut manger ces haricots.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 9: Le nouvel employé

Help a new employee learn how to be successful at your office. Answer her questions, using il faut.

Modèle

Est-ce qu'on est attentif au bureau?

Ah, oui. Il faut être attentif au bureau.

Commençons.
