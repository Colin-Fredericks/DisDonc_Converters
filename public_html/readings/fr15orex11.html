EXERCICE ORAUX 11-13

Exercice oral 11: Vous travaillez au restaurant

You have a new job at an elegant restaurant. Your boss is checking to see if you have set the tables properly. Answer each of his questions affirmatively by saying that you have put the items in the right place. Use the verb mettre and the corresponding direct object pronouns.

Modèle

Les nappes blanches sont sur la table?

Oui, je les ai mises sur la table.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 12: Quel mystère!

You and your roommate arrive home. Your roommate is shocked that certain things in the house are not closed. You echo his surprise and wonder who opened these things. Use a direct object pronoun and the passé composé of the verb ouvrir, to react.

Modèle

Regarde! La porte n'est pas fermée.

Mon Dieu. C'est vrai. Mais qui l'a ouverte?

Commençons.

Exercice oral 13: Le traiteur paresseux (The lazy caterer)

M. Boivin, a caterer in Paris, is checking with his partner to see if the dishes they need for today are ready. The partner has not yet made any of the dishes. Take the role of the partner.

Modèle

Tu as fait le pain?

Non, je ne l'ai pas encore fait.

Commençons.
