EXERCICES ORAUX 4-7

Exercice oral 4: Après le déménagement

What are people having to drink after they finish moving? Use the verb prendre to indicate their beverage of choice.

Modèle

Paul / de l'eau minérale

Paul prend de l'eau minérale.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 5: Aller chez Éliane

How are Éliane's friends getting to her house? Use the verb prendre and the cues provided to find out.

Modèle

Philippe / le Métro

Philippe prend le Métro.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 6: De bons stages

The speaker asks if various people are doing internships. Say that the person is, that the internship is going well, and that the person is learning a lot.

Modèle

Tu fais un stage maintenant?

Oui, c'est un bon stage. J'apprends beaucoup.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 7: Quel message!

Explain that none of the people mentioned understands the message.

Modèle

Daniel

Daniel ne comprend pas le message d'Émile.

Commençons.
