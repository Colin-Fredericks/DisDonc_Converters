Exercice oral 2: Quel arrondissement?

Ordinal numbers are also used to refer to the twenty arrondissements, the administrative divisions of the city of Paris. For example, you would say "Je travaille dans le huitième." ("I work in the eighth arrondissement."). Using the map below, answer the speaker's questions about where these people live. The speaker will be wrong in each case, so tell the speaker the correct arrondissement.
