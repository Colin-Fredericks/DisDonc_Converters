The final consonant sound of the stem of venir is n.

This final n drops in the singular, leaving a nasal vowel. Listen again to the pronunciation of the three singular forms.

The vowel e of the infinitive appears in the nous and vous forms: nous venons, vous venez. The singular and the third-person plural have the vowel ie:

je viens

tu viens

il vient

ils viennent

The endings of the singular forms are s, s, and t: je viens, tu viens, il vient.
