Le commissariat et l'Hôpital Saint-Louis

The police station, le commissariat, of the 19e arrondissement is located on the rue André Dubois, behind the Mairie, administrative offices, of the 19e. The Mairie is located next to the Parc des Buttes-Chaumont, a beautiful public park. The Hôpital Saint-Louis is a little over a kilometer to the west in the 10e arrondissement.

Le commissariat

In the Conversation, the police who interrogated Éliane are the police of the Préfecture de Paris, part of the police nationale, which is under a civil agency called Direction de l’administration de la police nationale. The gendarmes, which most foreigners think of as the typical French policemen, are actually a military force under the Ministère de la Défense, and also deal with threats to national security.
