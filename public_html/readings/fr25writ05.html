Exercice écrit 5

Translate the following phrases into French.

She blushed with (=de) shame.

I turned pale with fear.

Did you bleach the laundry?

I need brown socks.

The pages of the old book have turned yellow.

He took off his hat.

He’s wearing a dark blue suit.

Did you (vous) try on the clothing?

Yes, I put on the jacket.

She’s wearing a light green dress.
