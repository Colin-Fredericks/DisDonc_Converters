ORAL EXERCISES 7-9

Oral Exercise 7: Possessive Adjectives

Madame Duval has just moved to a new office. She checks with her assistant to make sure everything has arrived. Play the assistant's role. Use voilà in each case to show Madame Duval where things are.

Modèle

Où est mon ordinateur?

Voilà votre ordinateur, Madame.

Commençons.

Oral Exercise 8: Possessive Adjectives

Say where these people are. Use the cue you hear to answer the speaker's questions.

Modèle

à la fac

Votre professeur n'est pas là?

Non, notre professeur est à la fac.

Commençons.

Oral Exercise 9: Possessive Adjectives

Answer the following questions affirmatively, telling the speaker in each case that the things mentioned belong to Martine and Robert.

Modèle

Qu'est-ce que c'est?

L'agenda de Martine et Robert?

Oui, c'est leur agenda.

Commençons.
