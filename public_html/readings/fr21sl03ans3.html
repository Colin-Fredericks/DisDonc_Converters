ANSWERS

The reference pronoun en replaces a noun followed by a number. The noun may be either animate or inanimate.

Nous avons embauché trois vendeurs.

Nous en avons embauché trois.

The reference pronoun en is usually not translated in this context, although English sentences may use of them as an equivalent. However, the reference pronoun en is mandatory in French when used in reference to a noun preceded by a number.

The past participle does not agree with the reference pronoun en when en precedes the verb.

Il a renvoyé les stagiaires?

Pas tous. Il en a renvoyé trois.
