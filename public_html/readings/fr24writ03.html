Exercice écrit 4

Translate the following sentences into French.

Manquer

We miss them.

I miss her.

She misses me.

They’ll miss you (future; use vous).

He is not going to miss us. (immediate future)

He doesn’t miss his village in Switzerland anymore.

Il reste

We have nothing left.

They have enough money left.

I have twenty minutes (left).

How much money do you have left? (use vous)

He had one hundred dollars left. (use être to form the passé composé)

You had a month left. (use tu and the imperfect)
