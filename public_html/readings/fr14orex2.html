EXERCICES ORAUX 2-3

Exercice oral 2: Ne pas ouvrir

Say that the following people are not opening the items mentioned. Use the negative of the present tense of the verb ouvrir, to open.

Modèle

Pierre / son sac

Pierre n'ouvre pas son sac.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 3: On découvre la ville.

Say what the members of your tour group are discovering on their visit to a French city. Use the present tense of the verb découvrir, to discover.

Modèle

M. Martin / un quartier charmant

M. Martin découvre un quartier charmant.

Commençons.
