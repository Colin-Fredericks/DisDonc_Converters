EXERCICES ORAUX 8-9

Exercice oral 8: Jamais!

Say that people never do these things, using the reference pronoun en in your answers to the speaker’s questions.

Modèle

Ton copain boit du vin?

Non, il n’en boit jamais.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 9: Ça change

Confirm that the things the speaker asks about were true, but aren’t true anymore. Use si and the reference pronoun en twice in your answers to the speaker’s questions.

Modèle

Tu ne voulais pas de thé?

Si, j’en voulais, mais je n’en veux plus.

Commençons.
