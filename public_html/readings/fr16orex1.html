EXERCICES ORAUX 1-2

Exercice oral 1: Les communications au bureau

What do the people at the office receive? Form sentences with the present tense of the verb recevoir to say what each person gets.

Modèle

les informaticiens / des logiciels

Les informaticiens reçoivent des logiciels.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 2: Tout est déjà arrivé

The speaker is checking on the items he/she mailed. Respond in each case that the person to whom the items were mailed received them yesterday. Use the appropriate direct object pronoun and the passé composé of recevoir.

Modèle

Tu as envoyé la lettre à Marc Dubois?

Oui, il l'a reçue hier.

Commençons.
