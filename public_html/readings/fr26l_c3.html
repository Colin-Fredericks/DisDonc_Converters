LANGUE ET CULTURE 3

Les fringues et dingue

The words les fringues and dingue are slang words for vêtements and fou, respectively. These words are colloquial, and foreigners should be careful not to use them inappropriately. French slang is extremely rich – there are slang terms for almost everything. But the use of slang indicates a certain type of informal relationship and cannot be transferred to all situations.
