ORAL EXERCISES 4-6

 

Possessive Adjectives

Oral Exercise 4: Voilà

Your friend is checking to see if you have everything you need. Point out the things he asks about using voilà and the correct form of the possessive adjective.

Modèle

Tu as un stylo?

Oui, voilà mon stylo.

Commençons.

Oral Exercise 5: Où est?

Your friend is missing some of her things. You are surprised in each case that she doesn't have the item.

Modèle

Mais où est ma disquette?

Tu n'as pas ta disquette?

Commençons.

Oral Exercise 6: C'est

You and your friend are looking through some things that were left in the office and checking with each other to see to whom each item belongs. Answer your friend in each case with the correct possessive adjective.

Modèle

C'est le cahier de Philippe?

Oui, c'est son cahier.

Commençons.
