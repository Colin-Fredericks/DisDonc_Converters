EXERCICES ORAUX 10-11

Exercice oral 10: Mais on doit faire ça.

Using devoir, should, ought, have to, say that the following people should do the things they don't want to do.

Modèle

Nous ne voulons pas descendre.

Mais vous devez descendre.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 11: Comment?

Express surprise at what you hear, using Comment? State that the following people mustn't do the things they say they are going to do, using the correct form of devoir in the negative.

Modèle

Nous allons descendre.

Comment? Mais vous ne devez pas descendre.

Commençons.
