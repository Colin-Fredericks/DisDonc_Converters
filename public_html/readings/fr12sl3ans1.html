The endings are s, s, and t, just as they are in the singular of the present tense of the verb connaître. Note that these are the same endings as you see in -re verbs: je vends, tu vends, il vend, except that the ending -t is not written if the stem of the verb ends in d or t (il vend).

The final consonant sound of the stem is v as in the English word victory.

The v drops in the singular. In addition, the vowel of the singular forms is é (or è in careful speech).
