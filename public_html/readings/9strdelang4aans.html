ANSWERS

In the sentences with C’est / Ce sont, the words for nationality, religion, and profession are modified (they are accompanied by a determiner and/or by an adjective and therefore they function as nouns).

The adjectives of religion and nationality, such as protestant and chinois, are capitalized in these sentences because they function as nouns.

Des, the plural of the indefinite article is used after ce sont: Ce sont des Protestants pratiquants. Ce sont des Chinoises.
