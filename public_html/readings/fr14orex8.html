EXERCICES ORAUX 8-10

Exercice oral 8: Les appareils électriques

Say what appliances these people have plugged in. Use the verb brancher, to plug in, in the passé composé.

Modèle

Paul / son ordinateur

Paul a branché son ordinateur.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 9: Rien n'est fini.

The following people have not finished anything. Say so, using the verb finir, to finish, in the passé composé, and the phrase pas encore, not yet. Note that pas encore is placed between the auxiliary and the past participle.

Modèle

Baudoin / son dîner

Baudoin n'a pas encore fini son dîner.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 10: On ne fait pas attention!

Everyone on the trip has lost something. Say what they are missing, using the verb perdre, to lose, in the passé composé.

Modèle

Monsieur Lecompte / son portefeuille

Monsieur Lecompte a perdu son portefeuille.

Commençons.
