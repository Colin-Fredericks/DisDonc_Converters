ORAL EXERCISES 2-3

Oral Exercise 2: Au contraire

Tell the speaker that the person or thing asked about has the opposite characteristic.

Modèle

Ton frère est grand?

Non, il est petit.

Commençons.

Oral Exercise 3: La famille

Valérie is describing her family. Make the substitutions indicated to find out what her relatives are like.

Modèle

Ma mère est intelligente.

mon père

Mon père est intelligent.

mince

Mon père est mince.

Commençons.
