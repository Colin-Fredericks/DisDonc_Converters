The participles pris and écrit end in a consonant in writing. The participles mangé, choisi, and vendu end in a vowel.

The participles pris and écrit end in a pronounced consonant in the feminine forms; this consonant drops in the masculine forms. Thus, prise and prises end in a final z sound, which is dropped in the form pris. Similarly, the forms écrite and écrites end in a final t sound, which is dropped in the masculine forms écrit and écrits.
