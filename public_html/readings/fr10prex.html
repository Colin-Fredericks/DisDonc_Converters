EXERCICES DE PRONONCIATION

In the exercises below, give the alternate form of each of the adjectives you hear. The masculine forms end in a nasal vowel. The feminine forms end in the corresponding oral vowel + nasal consonant.

Exercice 1

Repeat each of the masculine forms after the speaker. When you hear the bell, say the corresponding feminine form. Repeat the correct response.

Modèle

Bon

Bell

Bonne

Commençons.

Exercice 2

Modèle

Bell

Bon
