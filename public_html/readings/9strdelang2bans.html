Combinations of de plus the definite article. The partitive article in French consists of de plus the definite article. The highlighted words are the partitive articles.

Du, as in the sentence: “J’ai du pâté.”

De la, as in the sentences: “Nous mangeons de la salade.” and “Elle écoute de la musique classique.”

De l’, as in the sentence: “Je fais de l’informatique.”
