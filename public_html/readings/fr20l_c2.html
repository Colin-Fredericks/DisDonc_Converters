LANGUE ET CULTURE 2

Education

The French translation of the English education can be tricky. The French word éducation usually means upbringing or breeding but can mean education in phrases such as le ministère de l’éducation nationale, the National Education Department. Often, English education is the equivalent of l’instruction as in l’instruction publique, public education.

The word education, especially in the sense of knowledge acquired is often la culture. The French equivalent of the English phrase well-educated is cultivé. In the sense of professional education, the French equivalent is usually la formation professionnelle.
