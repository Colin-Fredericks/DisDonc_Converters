Il faut faire un effort. means You have to try, one has to try, one must try, it is necessary to try.

Il faut manger le matin. means You have to eat in the morning, one must eat in the morning, it is necessary to eat in the morning.

Il faut faire le marché. means It is necessary to do the shopping, you have to do the shopping, one must do the shopping, people have to do the shopping.
