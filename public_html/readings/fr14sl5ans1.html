The plural forms of sortir (nous sortons, vous sortez, ils/elles sortent) do not have the iss of verbs like finir: nous finissons, vous finissez, ils/elles finissent.

The stem of the verb sortir is sort. The stem does not end in s as it does for verbs like finir whose stem is finiss.

The final consonant of the stem (t) drops in the singular forms: je sors, tu sors, il sort.

The endings of the singular are s, s, and t, as in most verbs whose infinitive does not end in er.
