ANSWERS

Nous sommes arrivés is used to refer to two or more males or to a group of two or more consisting of males and females. Nous sommes arrivées refers to two or more females.

Vous êtes arrivé is used in reference to one male that you address as vous.

Vous êtes arrivée is used in reference to one female that you address as vous. Vous êtes arrivés is used in reference to two or more males or to a group of two or more consisting of males and females. Vous êtes arrivées is used in reference to two or more females.
