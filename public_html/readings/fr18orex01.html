EXERCICES ORAUX 1-2

Exercice oral 1: La vérité

Respond to the speaker by stating that none of the following people is lying.

Modèle

Je crois qu'elle dit la vérité.

Moi aussi. Elle ne ment pas.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 2: Ce ne sont pas des mensonges.

React to each of the following statements, using the passé composé of mentir, to lie. The verb is used in this exercise to convey a meaning similar to that of I wasn't kidding, I was telling you the truth.

Modèle

Il a dit que Monsieur Jolivet était inquiet.

Il n'a pas menti.

Commençons.
