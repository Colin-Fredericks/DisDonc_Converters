LANGUE ET CULTURE 1

S. A. M. U.

The initials S. A. M. U. stand for Service d’Aide Médicale d’Urgence and are pronounced as if they were a word: “le samu.” Le S. A. M. U. is the French equivalent of emergency rescue services. They are reached by dialing 15.

La victime

The word la victime, like la personne, is always feminine in French, even when it refers to a man. That is why Éliane says, “Je connais la victime” when referring to Émile.

Le commissariat

Each arrondissement in Paris has a commissariat, police station.

Emmener

The verb emmener, to take someone away, take someone off, is conjugated like acheter, to buy. They both have a variable stem, with an open è in the singular and the third person plural. This is shown in French spelling by changing the e of the infinitive to è.

j’emmène

nous emmenons

tu emmènes

vous emmenez

il/elle/on emmène

ils/elles emmènent
