PRONUNCIATION EXERCISE 3

Plural to Singular (Masculine and Feminine)

Repeat the following plural nouns after the speaker. Make the article and the noun singular. Be sure to pronounce the vowel of the singular article distinctly in order to distinguish between the masculine singular, le, and the feminine singular, la.

Modèle

les cassettes/la cassette

Commençons.

les cartes

les francs

les copines

les lycées

les bureaux

les facs

les restaurants

les distributeurs

les serveuses

les voisins

les calculettes

les professeurs
