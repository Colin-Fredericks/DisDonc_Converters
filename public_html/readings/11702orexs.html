ORAL EXERCISES 1-4

Oral Substitution Exercise 1: Students

Ask the following people if they are students. Make sure to use étudiant and étudiante appropriately. Repeat the correct response after the speaker.

Modèle

Tu es étudiant, Marc?

Valérie

Tu es étudiante, Valérie?

Commençons.

Oral Substitution Exercise 2: Identifying Robert

Make the substitutions indicated to give information about Robert. Repeat the correct response after the speaker.

Modèle

Robert est canadien.

occupé

Robert est occupé.

Commençons.

Commençons.
