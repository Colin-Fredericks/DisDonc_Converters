EXERCICES ORAUX 10-12

Exercice oral 10: Au café

Say what these friends want to order at the café, using the correct forms of vouloir.

Modèle

Thérèse / un café

Thérèse veut un café.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 11: Pas seul!

No one wants to go to the appointment alone. Say so, using the correct forms of vouloir.

Modèle

Éliane

Éliane ne veut pas aller seule au rendez-vous.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 12: Aujourd'hui!

The speaker wants to know when people are going to do the things mentioned. Answer the questions by saying that they want to do these things today. Use the correct forms of vouloir.

Modèle

Quand est-ce que Martin va venir?

Il veut venir aujourd'hui.

Commençons.
