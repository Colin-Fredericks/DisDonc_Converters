EXERCICES ORAUX 12-13

Exercice oral 12: Questions avec est-ce que

Restate each pair of questions as a single, interrogative sentence using est-ce que.

Modèle

L'autobus arrive? D'où?

D'où est-ce que l'autobus arrive?

Commençons.

Exercice oral 13: Refaites vos questions!

Restate the questions you asked above in a more casual way by dropping est-ce que and placing the question word at the end of the sentence.

Modèle

D'où est-ce que l'autobus arrive?

L'autobus arrive d'où?

Commençons.
