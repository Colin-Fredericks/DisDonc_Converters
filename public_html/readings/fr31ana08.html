Autant que je sache, non. Mais j’ai des amis au Hameau, là-haut, qui m’ont parlé d’un groupe d’étrangers assez bizarres, parmi eux un homme énorme.

Not as far as I know. But I have friends in Le Hameau, up there, who spoke to me about a group of rather strange foreigners, among them a very big man.
