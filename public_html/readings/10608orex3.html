EXERCICES ORAUX 3-4

Exercice oral 3: Le verbe aller

State where the people mentioned are going, using the correct form of the verb aller and the destination provided. Remember to change the contractions of à + le to au and à + les to aux.

Modèle

vous/la bibliothèque

Vous allez à la bibliothèque.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 4: Où?

Answer the following questions about where people are going. Give a negative response, and then use the cue provided to correct the speaker's mistaken impression.

Modèle

fac/Jean va à la bibliothèque?

Non, il ne va pas a la bibliothèque. Il va à la fac.
