Oral Exercise 14: Simple Addition

Simple addition will help you practice French numbers. Look at each example and say it aloud. Click on the audio button for the correct answers. Repeat the correct response after the speaker.

Note the pattern for addition in French:

1 + 1 = 2

Un et un font deux.

 

Font means makes or equals.

Écoutez.

2 + 4 = 6

4 + 3 = 7

1 + 8 = 9

3 + 2 = 5

4 + 0 = 4

9 + 1 = 10
