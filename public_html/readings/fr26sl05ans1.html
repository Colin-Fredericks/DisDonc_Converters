Tout, when used as an adjective, has four forms in written French:

Singular

Plural

Masculine

tout

tous

Feminine

toute

toutes

When used as an adjective, tout has two forms in spoken French. The feminine singular and plural forms (toute, toutes) are pronounced alike and end in a pronounced t. That final t sound drops in the masculine singular and plural forms (tout, tous) that end in the vowel sound ou.
