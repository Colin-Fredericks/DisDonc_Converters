ORAL EXERCISES 11-13

Oral Exercise 11: Negation with Verbs

 

Contradict the speaker and say that the following people are not late. Be sure to drop the mute e of the word ne when necessary.

Modèle

Paul est en retard, n'est-ce pas?

Mais non. Il n'est pas en retard.

Commençons.

Oral Exercise 12: Occupé?

The speaker wants to know if the following people are busy. Answer in each case they're not, adding that that they don't have a lot of work at this time.

Modèle

Paul est très occupé, n'est-ce pas?

Non. Il n'a pas beaucoup de travail en ce moment.

Commençons.

Oral Exercise 13: Lunch Plans

The speaker is trying to confirm lunch plans. Unfortunately, all his notions about lunch are wrong. Tell him so by using the negative of the verb in each question.

Modèle

Nous mangeons en ville, n'est-pas?

Non, nous ne mangeons pas en ville.

Commençons.
