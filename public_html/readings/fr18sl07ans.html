The reflexive pronouns me, te, nous, and vous are the same as the corresponding direct and indirect object pronouns. What makes them reflexive is that they refer to the same person as the subject of the verb. For instance, in Tu te reposes, tu and te refer to the same person, the person you are talking to.

The reflexive pronoun for il, elle, and on is se. Remember that On se repose can mean either people rest or we rest.
