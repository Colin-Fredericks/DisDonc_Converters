EXERCICES ORAUX 5-6

Exercice oral 5: Qu’est-ce qui va se passer?

Say what is going to happen in our story, using the future tense.

Modèle

Paul / accompagner Éliane au bureau

Paul accompagnera Éliane au bureau.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 6: Les projets de Paul

Paul discusses what he thinks has to be done. Create sentences in the future tense from the elements you hear to find out what he says.

Modèle

je / soutenir Éliane

Je soutiendrai Éliane.

Commençons.
