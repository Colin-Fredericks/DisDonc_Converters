EXERCICE ORAL 11

Exercice oral 11: Pas encore

Ask if the following people have done certain tasks. When you hear the bell, say that the people are going to complete the tasks a little later (un peu plus tard). Use venir de + infinitive in your questions, and aller + infinitive in your answers.

Modèle

Pierre / faire la lessive

Pierre vient de faire la lessive?

Bell

Non. Il va faire la lessive un peu plus tard.

Commençons.
