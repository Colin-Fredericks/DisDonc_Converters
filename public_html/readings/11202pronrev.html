PRONUNCIATION REVIEW

Review How Nasal Vowels Are Signaled In French Spelling

The letters n and m in French often are not pronounced, but they indicate that the preceding vowel is nasal.

n and m before a different consonant signal that the preceding vowel is nasal.

bonjour

enchanté

étudiant

n and m at the end of a word signal that the preceding vowel is nasal.

canadien

coin

copain
