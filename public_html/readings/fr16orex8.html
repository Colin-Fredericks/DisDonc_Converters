EXERCICES ORAUX 8-10

Exercice oral 8: C'est le message

The answer to each of the following questions is le message. Answer the questions, using an indirect object pronoun to replace the animate noun of the question.

Modèle

Qu'est-ce qu'Émile a laissé à Éliane?

Il lui a laissé le message.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 9: Ils doivent avoir la clé!

Express your surprise that the following people were locked out. Use the passé composé of the verb and an indirect object pronoun to say that you made sure each of these people would have a key.

Modèle

(laisser) Robert n'a pas pu entrer.

Comment? Mais je lui ai laissé la clé.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 10: Solutions

Use the cues provided to express what to do in each of the following cases. Phrase your suggestion with il faut and the appropriate indirect object pronoun.

Modèle

(laisser un message) Nos amis ne sont pas là.

Il faut leur laisser un message.

Commençons.
