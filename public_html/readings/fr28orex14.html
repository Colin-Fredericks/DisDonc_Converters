EXERCICES ORAUX 14–15

Exercice oral 14: Nous ne risquons rien

Mr. Jolivet tries to reassure his staff about the situation at CyberAvenir. Create sentences from the cues you hear, using aucun/aucune + de + plural noun. Note that the verbs in these sentences are third-person singular, agreeing with aucun/aucune.

Modèle

nos projets / être en danger

Aucun de nos projets n’est en danger.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 15: Ni l’un ni l’autre

Use ni . . . ni, followed by a singular verb, to say that neither of the people or things the speaker asks about is doing the following things.

Modèle

Qui travaille aujourd’hui? Julien? Karim?

Non, ni Julien ni Karim ne travaille aujourd’hui.

Commençons.
