EXERCICES ORAUX 1-3

Exercice oral 1: Qu'est-ce qu'il y a pour le dîner?

Your friend wants to make sure you have everything you need for dinner. Reassure your friend that you have a lot of each thing mentioned, using beaucoup de, a lot of.

Modèle

On a des pommes de terre?

Mais, oui. On a beaucoup de pommes de terre.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 2: Avant de faire les courses

Before going out to do the food shopping, the speaker checks to see what you need. Tell the speaker that you have enough of each item, using assez de, enough of.

Modèle

Tu as besoin de cerises?

Non, j'ai assez de cerises.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 3: Trop, c'est trop

You are showing the speaker around your apartment. The speaker comments on the things you have a lot of. You tell the speaker you have too many of them, using trop de, too many, too much of.

Modèle

Je vois que tu as beaucoup d'affiches.

Beaucoup d'affiches? J'ai trop d'affiches.

Commençons.
