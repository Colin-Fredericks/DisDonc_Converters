The adjective bon ends in a nasal vowel only in sentence 1: C’est un bon quartier.

Bon is pronounced with an open o and a final consonant.

The adjective bon in sentences 2 and 3 is pronounced like the feminine form bonne in sentences 4 and 5.

As is true with beau, nouveau, and vieux, the feminine form of the adjective bon is used before masculine nouns beginning with a vowel or silent h. However, in written French, no special form of bon is used to indicate this. Compare C’est un bon exercice with C’est un nouvel exercice.
