PRONUNCIATION EXERCISE 2

Singular to Plural

Repeat the following singular nouns after the speaker. Make the article and the noun plural. Be sure to pronounce the vowel of the plural article les as é, as in French café. If the noun begins with a vowel, remember to make liaison.

Modèle

la cassette/les cassettes

Commençons.

le vin

le chocolat

l'imprimante

la limonade

l'addition

le billet

la quiche

la serveuse

l'ordinateur

la terrasse

l'agenda

le serveur
