There are two forms of the adjective in speech: the masculine singular intéressant and the masculine plural intéressants are pronounced one way while the feminine singular intéressante and the feminine plural intéressantes are pronounced another way. The feminine forms in speech end in the consonant t. This t is dropped in the masculine forms, which end in a nasal vowel.

The adjective intéressant has four forms in writing:
