LANGUE ET CULTURE 1

 

Sortir

 

Note that sortir can be used as a transitive verb (a transitive verb has a direct object), with the meaning of to take out. Several verbs of motion have this property, as you will learn in the next Préparation.

 

 

Interrompre

 

The verb interrompre is conjugated like rompre.

 

Present tense

 

j’interromps

nous interrompons

tu interromps

vous interrompez

il/elle/on interrompt

ils/elles interrompent

 

The past participle is interrompu.

 

j’ai interrompu

nous avons interrompu

tu as interrompu

vous avez interrompu

il/elle/on a interrompu

ils/elles ont interrompu

 

Interrompre, like rompre, is a regular -re verb.
