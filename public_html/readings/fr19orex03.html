EXERCICES ORAUX 3-4

Exercice oral 3: Personne n’est debout

The speaker will ask you if the following people are standing. Answer in each case that they have already sat down.

Modèle

Maurice est debout?

Non, il s’est assis.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 4: Je peux m’asseoir?

Answer the following questions in the affirmative. The verbs used with s’asseoir are vouloir and pouvoir. Remember that the reflexive pronoun used with an infinitive changes to agree with the subject of the sentence.

Modèle

Il veut s’asseoir?

Oui, il veut s’asseoir.

Commençons.
