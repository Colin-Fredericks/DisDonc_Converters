EXERCICES ORAUX 14-17

Exercice oral 14: On demande son chemin

A tourist has just asked you for directions to the law school at the local university. Modify the sentences you hear and give the directions to him in the vous form of the imperative.

Modèle

Vous pouvez prendre l'autobus.

Prenez l'autobus.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 15: La visite

Baudoin's aunt Josette is getting the house ready for a family visit. She tells her son, Théophile, her daughter, Françoise, and her husband, Christophe, what to do. Use the tu form of the imperative to give commands.

Modèle

Tes livres, Françoise. Il faut les ranger.

Françoise, range tes livres, s'il te plaît.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 16: Ne faites pas ça!

Aunt Josette sees the children doing things they shouldn't do. What negative command would she use in each of the following situations? Use a singular or plural command depending on whom she addresses.

Modèle

Théophile mange la tarte aux fraises.

Théophile! Ne mange pas la tarte aux fraises.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 17: Le rendez-vous avec Émile

Paul and Daniel are discussing with Régine how to get to the café Au Relais du Canal. They ask Régine for exact information, and she answers them in each case with a nous-form command.

Modèle

À quelle station est-ce qu'il faut aller? À Chemin Vert?

Oui, allons à Chemin Vert.

Commençons.
