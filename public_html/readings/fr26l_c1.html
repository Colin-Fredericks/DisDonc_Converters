LANGUE ET CULTURE 1

La matinée

In French, remember that the words day, morning, and evening can serve a dual meaning – they can express both a point in time and the events of the period.

Point in Time

Events of the Period

Day

le jour

la journée

Morning

le matin

la matinée

Evening

le soir

la soirée

The words journée, matinée, and soirée express the entire duration of a period of time.

The words l’après-midi and la nuit express a point in time and particular events of a period.

Notice that la matinée can also mean afternoon show and la soirée can mean evening party.
