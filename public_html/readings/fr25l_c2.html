LANGUE ET CULTURE 2

Une veste

Note that with articles of clothing, much like with parts of the body, French uses the singular when referring to an article of clothing.

Les garçons apportent une veste.

The boys bring jackets.

Montre en or

Note that in French the material used to make something is usually expressed with the preposition en.

un pull en laine

a woolen sweater

une bague en or

a gold ring

If, however, the material mentioned is only part of the whole, then the preposition de is used.

un collier de perles

a pearl necklace

un bracelet de diamants

a diamond bracelet
