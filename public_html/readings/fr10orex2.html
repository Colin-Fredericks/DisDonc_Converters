Exercice oral 2: Changez la forme

Listen to the following phrases. If you hear a third-person plural form, say the corresponding third-person singular form. Conversely, if you hear a third-person singular form, give the corresponding third-person plural form.

Modèle

il attend

ils attendent

il attend

il attend

Commençons.

Exercice oral 3: Un bruit!

Each of the following people or groups of people hears a noise. Say so, using the correct form of the verb entendre, to hear.

Modèle

Pierre

Pierre entend un bruit.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 4: Meubles à vendre

The following people are selling their furniture. Say so, using the correct form of the verb vendre, to sell, and the corresponding possessive adjective.

Modèle

Marie

Marie vend ses meubles.

Commençons.
