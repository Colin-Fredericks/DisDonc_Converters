EXERCICES ORAUX 13-14

Exercice oral 13: Tout le monde est prêt (ou presque)!

Cousin Françoise explains what everyone at her house is doing to prepare for an outing. Create sentences, using the verb finir, to finish.

Modèle

papa / son travail

Papa finit son travail.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 14: Au marché

Say what people are selecting at the market, using choisir, to choose.

Modèle

Éliane / des tomates

Éliane choisit des tomates.

Commençons.
