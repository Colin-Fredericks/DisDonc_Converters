La DST

La Direction de la surveillance du territoire (DST) is a branch of the French police force that was established to protect the country from foreign threats. The DST safeguards France from foreign terrorists, spies, and attempts to harm French science, industry, or technology.

Interpol

First instituted in 1956, Interpol is an international police force comprised of 177 countries that belong to Interpol. The organization's mission is to coordinate efforts among police departments from different countries to combat and capture criminals who operate between countries.
