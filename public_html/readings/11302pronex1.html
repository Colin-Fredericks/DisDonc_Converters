PRONUNCIATION EXERCISE 1

You will hear the feminine form of an adjective of nationality read twice. In each case, give the corresponding masculine form of the adjective by deleting the final consonant of the feminine form. If the final consonant sound in the feminine form is n, form the masculine by deleting the final n sound and nasalizing the preceding vowel. The speaker will provide the correct response. Repeat the correct response after the speaker.
