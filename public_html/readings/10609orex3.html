EXERCICES ORAUX 3-4

Exercice oral 3: Qu'est-ce qu'on apporte?

What are people bringing to the picnic? Use the verb apporter, to bring, and the partitive article to find out.

Modèle

Roger / jambon

Roger apporte du jambon.
