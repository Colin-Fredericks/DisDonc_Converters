The irregular stem is aill-. It is pronounced the way -aille is pronounced in travaille. 

The irregular stem aill- is used in the three forms of the singular (que j’aille, que tu ailles, qu’il/elle aille) and in the third-person plural (qu’ils/elles aillent). 

The other forms of the subjunctive (the nous- and vous-forms) use the stem of the infinitive that appears in the present indicative:

present indicative

present subjunctive

nous allons

que nous allions

vous allez

que vous alliez
