EXERCICES ORAUX 8–9

Exercice oral 8: C’est à quelqu’un d’autre

Tell the speaker that the items do not belong to the person mentioned.

Modèle

C’est le livre de Julien?

Non, ce n’est pas le sien.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 9: L’arrivée de l’autocar touristique

The tourist bus has arrived, and all the items in the luggage compartment have been placed in the hotel lobby. The guide checks to see to whom they belong. Answer in each case, using an interrogative pronoun, a demonstrative pronoun, and a possessive pronoun as in the model.

Modèle

C’est la valise de M. et Mme Jolivet?

Laquelle? Celle-là? Non, celle-là n’est pas la leur.

Commençons.
