EXERCICES ORAUX 8-9

Exercice oral 8: Notre week-end

Robert is talking about what he and his friends are doing this weekend. Change the subject of each sentence from nous to on to reflect informal speech.

Modèle

Nous louons un film.

On loue un film.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 9: Au bureau

Nicole is showing some visitors around the lycée. Change the subject of each sentence from on to nous to reflect informal speech.

Modèle

On travaille ici.

Nous travaillons ici.

Commençons.
