The three similarities are as follows:

Vouloir has a variable stem. The vowel of the infinitive appears only in the nous and vous forms:

vouloir

nous voulons

vous voulez

In the singular and the third-person plural the vowel of the stem is eu:

je veux

tu veux

il/elle/on veut

ils/elles veulent

Vouloir loses the final consonant of its stem in the singular. Compare the forms ils veulent and il veut.

Like pouvoir, vouloir ends in x rather than s in the je and tu forms:

je veux

tu veux
