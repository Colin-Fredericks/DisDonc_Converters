The adjective prochain ends in a nasal vowel only in example 1: le prochain message.

Prochain is pronounced with an open e and a final consonant.

The adjective prochain in examples 2 and 3 is pronounced like the feminine form prochaine in examples 4 and 5.

Like bon, the spoken feminine form prochain is used before masculine nouns beginning with a vowel or silent h. However, in written French, no special form of prochain is used to indicate this. Listen again, comparing le prochain arrêt with la prochaine rue.
