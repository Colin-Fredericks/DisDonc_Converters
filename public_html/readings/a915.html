on vient d’arriver

we have just arrived

On vient d’arriver à Paris.

We’ve just arrived in Paris.

compter

to count; to intend, to plan

on compte rester

we plan to remain, we expect to stay

On compte rester deux ans ici.

We plan to stay here for two years.

Daniel et moi, on vient d’arriver à Paris. On compte rester deux ans ici.

Daniel and I have just arrived in Paris. We plan to stay here for two years.
