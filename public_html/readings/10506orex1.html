ORAL EXERCISES 1-2

Oral Exercise 1: Visite de la ville

You are showing a French friend around your city. Point out the following places and describe them using the adjective provided. Remember that since all French nouns are either masculine or feminine, they are replaced by il or elle (plural: ils or elles) when they are the subject of the sentence.

Modèle

la cathédrale/magnifique

Voilà la cathédrale. Elle est magnifique.

Commençons.

Oral Exercise 2: Qu'est-ce que tu vois?

Describe what you see using il y a, the noun, and the prepositional phrase provided.

Modèle

un journal/sur la chaise

Il y a un journal sur la chaise.

Commençons.
