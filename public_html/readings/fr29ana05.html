(29.5)

exagérer

to exaggerate, overdo it

guetter

to lurk, loom, watch over

le danger qui nous guette

the danger looming over us

prévenir

to take precautions, prevent

guérir

to cure

Mieux vaut prévenir que guérir.

Better to prevent illness than to cure it. (English equivalent: An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure.)

faire attention

to be careful

Et moi je suis sûr que tu exagères le danger qui nous guette. Mais tu as raison. Mieux vaut prévenir que guérir. On fera attention.

And I am sure you’re exaggerating the danger looming over us. But you’re right. An ounce of prevention is worth a pound of cure. We’ll be careful.
