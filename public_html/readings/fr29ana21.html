(29.21)

s’enfuir

to escape, get away

Il s’est enfui.

He got away.

l’alerte

alert, alarm

l’alerte générale

all-points bulleting

installer

to install, set up

le barrage

roadblock

Non, il s’est enfui. Mais nous avons donné l’alerte générale et installé des barrages partout.

No, he got away. But we sent out an all points bulletin and set up roadblocks everywhere.
