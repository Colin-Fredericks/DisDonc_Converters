Conférence et assister

The words conférence and assister are examples of false cognates, related words in English and French that do not mean the same thing in the two languages.

The most common meaning of conférence is lecture. Conference in the sense of a convention is le congrès or colloque. Conference in the sense of a meeting is une réunion. However, a press conference is une conférence de presse and the expression être en conférence does mean to be in a meeting.

The most common meaning of assister à is to attend. The meaning to assist, however, does exist in phrases such as assister les pauvres, to assist/minister to the poor. The derived form l’assistant(e) means assistant.

Attendre

Remember that the French cognate for attend, attendre, means to wait for.
