LANGUE ET CULTURE 3

Au bar

The highest prices are for beverages consumed outdoors à la terrasse. The intermediate level is for drinks consumed à l’intérieur. Traditionally, one can sit at a café with one beverage for as long as one likes. Some very popular cafés in crowded areas require customers to reorder every two hours. The sign that notifies customers of this rule reads as follows: Prière de renouveler la consommation toutes les deux heures.
