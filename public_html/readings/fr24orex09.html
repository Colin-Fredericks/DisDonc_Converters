EXERCICES ORAUX 9-10

Exercice oral 9: Quand on se verra

Use the future tense to say when you will see each other. Eliminate the subordinate clause and replace it with the noun that corresponds to the verb.

Modèle

On se verra quand on partira.

On se verra à notre départ.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 10: Qu’est-ce qu’on attend?

Say what people are waiting for. Replace the subordinate clauses with que by the correponding noun. Note that attendre que is followed by the subjunctive.

Modèle

On attend que le médecin arrive.

On attend l’arrivée du médecin.

Commençons.
