Exercice écrit 4

Select the correct English translation for each of the following sentences.

1.

Il y a un quart d'heure qu'Émile attend au café.

a.

Émile was waiting at the café fifteen minutes ago.

b.

Émile has been waiting at the café for fifteen minutes.

2.

Il y dix minutes qu'il pleut des cordes.

a.

It's been pouring for ten minutes.

b.

It poured ten minutes ago.

3.

La police ont repêché Émile il y a cinq minutes.

a.

The police have been getting Émile out of the water for five minutes.

b.

The police got Émile out of the water five minutes ago.

4.

Il y a un an que quelqu'un essaie de voler le nouveau système de CyberAvenir.

a.

A year ago someone was trying to steal CyberAvenir's new system.

b.

Someone has been trying to steal CyberAvenir's new system for a year.

5.

On a appelé le S. A. M. U. il y a une demi-heure.

a.

They've been calling the S. A. M. U. for half an hour.

b.

They called the S. A. M. U. half an hour ago.
