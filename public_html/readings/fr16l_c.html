LANGUE ET CULTURE

Télécommunication

English terms are often used alongside French terms for many aspects of telecommunications and mass marketing. Here are some examples:

le publipostage

le mailing

le courrier électronique

l’e-mail

le Réseau

le Web

la gestion

le marketing

Note that in French demander une question is not the equivalent of to ask a question in English. With question, the verb poser is used: poser une question à quelqu’un.
