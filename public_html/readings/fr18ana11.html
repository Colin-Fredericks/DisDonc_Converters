(18.11)

se détendre

to relax

te détendre

to relax (with tu as the subject)

Tu dois te détendre.

You must relax.

Tu dois rentrer te détendre.

You must go home to relax.

se coucher

to go to bed

te coucher

to go to bed (with tu as the subject)

Tu dois te coucher.

You ought to go to bed.

Tu dois rentrer te coucher.

You ought to go home and go to bed.

la force

strength

les forces

one’s personal strength

le bout

end

à bout de forces

all worn out

être à bout de forces

to be all worn out

se lever

to get up

tu te lèves

you get up

tu t’es levée

you got up (note the past participle agreement of the pronoun te and the feminine subject, Éliane)

Tu t’es levée si tôt.

You got up so early.

si peu

so little

Tu as mangé si peu.

You’ve eaten so little.

Tu dois rentrer te détendre un peu, te coucher. Tu es à bout de forces. Tu t’es levée si tôt ce matin et tu as mangé si peu.

You should go home to relax a little, to go bed. You’re all worn out. You got up so early this morning and you’ve eaten so little.
