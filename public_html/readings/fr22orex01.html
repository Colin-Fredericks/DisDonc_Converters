EXERCICES ORAUX 1-2

Exercice oral 1: Mes collègues

The speaker mentions certain qualities that he feels his co-workers lack. Respond in each case using C’est dommage, That’s too bad. Agree that those co-workers require those qualities for their work, using il faut plus the corresponding noun.

Modèle

Roland n’est pas très courageux.

C’est dommage. Pour son travail, il faut beaucoup de courage.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 2: Ce qu’il faut faire

Using il faut, respond to the speaker’s questions by saying that you have to do the following things.

Modèle

Alors, vous allez partir?

Oui. Il me faut partir.

Commençons.
