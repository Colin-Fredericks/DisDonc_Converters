Oral Exercise 16: Multiplication

Practicing multiplication will help you learn French numbers. Look at each example and say it aloud. Click on the audio button for the correct answers. Repeat the correct response after the speaker.

Note the pattern for multiplication in French.

6 x 6 = 36

Six fois six font trente-six.

Fois means times. Font means makes or equals.
