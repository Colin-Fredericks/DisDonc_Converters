EXERCICES ORAUX 1-2

Exercice oral 1:Sylvie change d’adresse

You’re surprised to learn from your friend that Sylvie has moved. React to each piece of news you hear by saying Qui t’a dit que . . . ? and using the pluperfect. All of the following verbs form their pluperfect with avoir. Note that one of the uses of the pluperfect is in indirect discourse.

Modèle

Sylvie a déménagé.

Qui t’a dit que Sylvie avait déménagé?

Commençons.

Exercice oral 2:Tout le monde est au courant

Use the pluperfect of the verbs given to find out how people learned about the following things.

Modèle

remarquer

Tu savais que M. Jolivet était exaspéré?

Oui, je l'avais remarqué.

Commençons.
