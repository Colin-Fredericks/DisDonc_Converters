EXERICICES ORAUX 1-2

Exercices oral 1: Éliane s’exprime

Éliane is thinking about what has happened over the last few days. Discover what she thinks about the events by constructing sentences with the verbs and expressions given.

Modèle

Mes copains me soutiennent. / Je suis contente que

Je suis contente que mes copains me soutiennent.

Commençons.

Exercices oral 2: Avec tout ce qui se passe

Paul is thinking about the situation at CyberAvenir. Express his thoughts by combining the two sentences you hear into a single sentence containing an impersonal expression. Remove c’est/ça in the original statement and replace the expression with il est/il.

Modèle

Baudoin vient au bureau. C’est curieux, ça.

Il est curieux que Baudoin vienne au bureau.

Commençons.
