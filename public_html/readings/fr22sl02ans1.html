The tu-form of the subjunctive is the same as the indicative in -er verbs:

Indicative

Tu montes.

Subjunctive

Il faut que tu montes.

In the tu-form of the subjunctive the final consonant of the stem is pronounced; it is not pronounced in the indicative. 

The tu-form of the subjunctive derives from the third-person plural of the present indicative:

ils éclaircissent

il faut que tu éclaircisses

ils descendent

il faut que tu descendes

ils viennent

il faut que tu viennes
