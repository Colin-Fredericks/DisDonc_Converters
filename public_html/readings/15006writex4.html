WRITING EXERCISE 4

1. Comment sont tes amis? (What are your friends like?)

Describe your friends using some of the descriptive adjectives you learned in this lesson, such as intelligent, gentil, drôle, actif, etc.

2. Describe the town or neighborhood you live in by saying what places there are and where they are in relation to each other. Describe what places you and/or your friends like or dislike.
