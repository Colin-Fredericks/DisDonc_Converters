EXERCICES ORAUX 9-10

Exercice oral 9: On ne le fait pas

People are not cooperating today. Say so by answering the speaker's questions in the negative, and replacing the direct object nouns with the corresponding pronouns.

Modèle

Paul accroche les affiches?

Non, il ne les accroche pas.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 10: Non

Tell the speaker that you are not going to do what he or she suggests.

Modèle

Ce roman est très ennuyeux. Tu comptes le lire?

Non, je ne vais pas le lire.

Commençons.
