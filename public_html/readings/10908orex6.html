EXERCICES ORAUX 6-7

Exercice oral 6: Changez à est-ce que?

Change each of these questions, asked with change of intonation only, to est-ce que questions. Change the subject from tu to vous as well.

Modèle

Tu vas manger en ville?

Est-ce que vous allez manger en ville?

Commençons.

Exercice oral 7: On cherche un appartement

You are looking for an apartment in Paris and have found one that seems suitable. Use est-ce que to turn these statements into questions for the real estate agent.

Modèle

L'appartement est moderne.

Est-ce que l'appartement est moderne?

Commençons.
