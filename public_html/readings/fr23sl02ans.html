The stem with open e is used.

The stem with open e is used before a syllable containing a mute e. In all the forms of the future, the e of the infinitive of -er verbs is pronounced as a mute e. 

The nous and vous forms use the stem with mute e in the present, but the stem with open e in the future. Thus, the variable stem verbs, like all other verbs, have the same stem in all forms of the future. 

All the future forms of verbs ending in -yer end in i, not y, because these verbs change y to i before a mute e, and in all the forms of the future, the e of the infinitive of -er verbs is pronounced as a mute e: je balaierai, nous nettoierons, vous essuierez.
