LANGUE ET CULTURE

Devenir

Devenir, to become, is conjugated like venir. The passé composé is formed with être.

De plus en plus

Note the use of de plus en plus before an adjective to mean more and more.

Vous autres

The forms vous autres and nous autres exist in spoken French as pronouns stressing the plural.

Agir

Be sure to distinguish agir, to act, a fully conjugated verb, from the idiom il s’agit de, it is about / it is a question of, which only appears in the third-person singular and is reflexive.
