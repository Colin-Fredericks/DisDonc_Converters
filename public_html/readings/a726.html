Everyone is at our house.

birthday

Aujourd’hui tout le monde est chez nous. C’est l’anniversaire de tata Josette. Bon anniversaire, tata Josette!

Today everyone is at our house. It’s Aunt Josette’s birthday. Happy birthday, Aunt Josette.
