EXERCICES ORAUX 7-10

Exercice oral 7: Mon copain

Repeat each sentence after the speaker. When you hear the bell, rephrase each sentence to describe your friend (mon copain), using a subordinate clause introduced by qui. Begin each rephrased sentence with J'ai un copain qui.

Modèle

Mon copain est très travailleur.

Bell

J'ai un copain qui est très travailleur.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 8: Notre professeur

Repeat each sentence after the speaker. When you hear the bell, rephrase each sentence to describe Monsieur Lamoureux, a well-liked professor, using a subordinate clause introduced by que.

Modèle

Nous respectons ce professeur.

Bell

Voilà Monsieur Lamoureux. C'est un professeur que nous respectons.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 9: La chambre de Régine

Régine is describing her room. Repeat each sentence; then, when you hear the bell, agree with her by rephrasing her sentence to create a subordinate clause that modifies chambre and is introduced by qui.

Modèle

Ma chambre coûte les yeux de la tête.

Bell

C'est vrai. Tu as une chambre qui coûte les yeux de la tête.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 10: L'appartement de Philippe

Philippe is telling you what he is going to do in his apartment. Repeat each sentence; then, when you hear the bell, confirm each of his plans by rephrasing each sentence so that it includes a relative clause beginning with que.

Modèle

Je vais décorer la chambre.

Bell

Ah, voici la chambre que tu vas décorer.

Commençons.
