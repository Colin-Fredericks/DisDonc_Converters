EXERCICES ORAUX 1-3

Exercice oral 1: On sort

Describe Paul and Solange’s date, from the arrangements to the meeting. Use the passé composé and the reciprocal reflexive of each verb given.

Modèle

parler

Ils se sont parlé.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 2: Un rendez-vous manqué, ou presque

A young man is talking about a date that almost didn’t work out because he and the girl he asked out nearly missed each other. Use the colloquial construction on s’est and the passé composé to respond.

Modèle

se donner rendez-vous

On s’est donné rendez-vous.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 3: Un amour brisé

Answer the speaker’s questions about a couple that has broken up. In each case, say that the couple used to do caring things for one another, though they don't do these things anymore.

Modèle

Ils se voient encore?

Ils se voyaient, mais ils ne se voient plus.

Commençons.
