PRONONCIATION PRACTICE 2

Basic Intonation Patterns (2)

Compare these two questions:

Is John here?

Where is John?

Which of the questions can be answered by either yes or no?

Which of the questions asks for information, and cannot be answered by yes or no?

Now say those two questions aloud.

Did your voice rise or fall at the end of the first one?

Did your voice rise or fall at the end of the second one?

In English, information questions have falling intonation, not rising intonation. In French, as in English, information questions are spoken with falling intonation. Listen to the speaker read the following information questions in French.

Écoutez.

Combien d'euros?

Qu'est-ce que tu as?

Où est mon copain?

Qui est Paul?

Did the speaker's voice rise or fall?
