(28.24)

rire

to laugh

faire rire

make (people) laugh

qui fait rire

funny (humorous)

C’est une histoire qui fait rire.

It’s a funny story.

pour rire

to laugh, as a joke

Je disais ça pour rire.

I didn’t mean it/I meant it as a joke.

un éclat

burst

elle riait

she laughed

Elle riait aux éclats.

She roared with laughter.

rigolo (colloquial)

funny

C’est rigolo.

It’s funny.

rigoler (colloquial)

to laugh, have fun

ce que + subject pronoun and verb

how, how much

Ce qu’on a rigolé au café!

Did we have fun at the café!

rira

will laugh

le dernier

last one

qui rira le dernier

the one who will laugh last

Rira bien qui rira le dernier.

He who laughs last, laughs best.
