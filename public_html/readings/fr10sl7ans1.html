The feminine forms belle, nouvelle, and vieille all end in lle.

The forms belle and nouvelle rhyme. The sound at the end is similar to that of the English word bell. The feminine form vieille, however, ends in a sound similar to the y of the English word yellow. This is a common pronunciation of the letters lle after the letter i in French; compare vieille to travaille.
