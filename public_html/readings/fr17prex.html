EXERCICES DE COMPRÉHENSION

Exercice de compréhension 1

Now see if you can hear the difference between the passé composé and the imparfait. For each verb phrase you hear, indicate the tense.

Écoutez.

ANSWERS

In everyday speech, the distinction in pronunciation between closed and open e at the end of a word is often blurred. This means that the difference between the passé composé and the imperfect of the je form of -er verbs conjugated with avoir is indicated solely by the vowel of the first syllable. Listen to the informal pronunciation of these forms:

Écoutez.

j'ai parlé

je parlais

j'ai chanté

je chantais

j'ai développé

je développais
