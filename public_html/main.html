Welcome to Dis Donc!

Dis Donc is an online textbook for introductory French language learning. It includes a not just text, but audio and video to help you learn French more effectively.

To get started, go to Préparacion 1.

About the Author

Marlies Mueller received her Ph.D. in Romance Languages and Literatures from Harvard University in 1974. She went on to teach at Harvard University and has prepared graduate students and teaching assistants to teach all courses in the French language program. The author of Les Idées politiques dans le roman heroïques de 1630 a 1670 and Militarism and Pacifism in the Ancien Régime, Dr. Mueller has also published numerous articles and chapters in multi-author books, and has been featured in NTC Publishing and Harvard's Derek Bok Center videotapes and interactive videodiscs on teacher training.

About the New Version

DisDonc was originally distributed on three DVDs, using Adobe Flash. It was converted to HTML5 in 2023 by Colin Fredericks, with advice and oversight from Dr. Kimberlee Campbell of Harvard. Every attempt has been made to preserve the original content with a new layout, updated facts and figures, and improved accessibility for screen readers and other assistive technologies.
