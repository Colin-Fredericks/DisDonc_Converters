EXERCICES ORAUX 13-16

Exercice oral 13: Oh, l'ennui. . .

Explain that the following people are not taking any classes, because they are bored at college.

Modèle

Caroline ne suit pas de cours.

Elle s'ennuie à la fac.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 14: Oh, le repos. . .

Answer the following questions regarding what people are doing. Say that they are resting.

Modèle

Qu'est-ce qu'il fait maintenant, Jean-Pierre? Il travaille?

Non, il se repose.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 15: Oh, la fatigue. . .

When you hear that the following people are sleepy, agree that they are sleepy and say that they should go to bed.

Modèle

Jean-Marc a sommeil.

Oui, c'est vrai. Il doit se coucher.

Commençons.

Exercice oral 16: On va s'amuser

The speaker tells you that the following people are going to Paris. Respond by saying that that they are going to have a good time.

Modèle

Christine va passer ses vacances à Paris.

Elle va s'amuser.

Commençons.
