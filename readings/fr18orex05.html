EXERCICES ORAUX 5-6

Exercice oral 5: Interrogez!

Imagine that you have led the espionage investigation at M. Jolivet's firm. A detective tells you what he wants to find out about Émile's duties. Use inversion to phrase each question.

Modèle

Je veux savoir s'il lave le plancher.

Lave-t-il le plancher?

Commençons.

Exercice oral 6: Vous interrogez Éliane.

Pretend that you are the policeman who is asking Éliane some preliminary questions in preparation for the interrogation. Ask each question in two ways.

Ask an affirmative question.

After the speaker tells you to ask the question again, using the command Redemandez à Éliane. . . (Ask Éliane again. . . .), ask for the same information, using a negative question. Use inversion in both.

Modèle

Demandez à Éliane si elle est fatiguée.

Êtes-vous fatiguée?

Redemandez à Éliane si elle est fatiguée.

N'êtes-vous pas fatiguée?

Commençons.
