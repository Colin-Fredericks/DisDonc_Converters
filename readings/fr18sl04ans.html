ANSWERS

In sentence a, the policeman asks Éliane, “Êtes-vous sûre, Mademoiselle?” This directly quoted question is in the present tense.

In sentence b, the indirectly quoted question is reported in the imperfect tense. Questions originally asked in the present are usually reported in the imperfect tense.

Questions asked to tu or vous are reported in the third person: il, elle, ils, elles. The reference to the third person appears in the quoting of the direct question.
